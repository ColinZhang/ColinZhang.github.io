Bio

I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the Cornell University. I am advised by Prof. Ari Juels and I am a member of IC3. My research interest spans systems security, cryptocurrencies, trusted hardware and their intersection. Before I joint Cornell, I got my bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University.
